AKAN NAMES



DESCRIPTION

This website takes you birthday and calculates the day of the week you were born and then depending on your gender outputs your Akan Name.

Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture. Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born.

These akan names are believed to give a deeper meaning of the person's character and soul
